Café Under Masken

Cozy and mysterious

What?

Under Masken, in English “Under the Mask”, in a small cafe in the basement of Hotel Royal, located on the street Bispegade in Aarhus. The fact the founder is an artist clearly shows in the decor.
The place is covered in paint and sculptures, having a cozy atmosphere even with the clutter.

Why?

Hans Krull, a very passionate painter and sculptor, is the one who created UnderMasken. He says his intention for this cafe is it to be a place for everyone, and a place that attracts people with its “mood”.
Most of the people who frequent Under Masken are students and creative professionals alike.

Who?

Under Masken was opened by Hans Krull. Another great creation of his in Aarhus is the Seagull Kiss mural on street Fiskergade.
After 27 years of ownership, he sold the establishment to Tom Hvas, an old acquaintance of his and the owner of many pubs around Aarhus, after he told Hans he is interested in buying it.

When and where?

Opening hours: Monday-Wednesday 12:00-02:00, Thursday-Friday 12:00-03:00, Saturday 13:00-03:00, Sunday 13:00-00:00
Address: Bispegade 3, 8000 Aarhus
Transport access: Walking, autovehicle, micromobiles (eg: bike)
