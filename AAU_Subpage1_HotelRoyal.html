Hotel Royal

Grandious and luxurious

What?

Hotel Royal is the oldest hotel in Aarhus, it was opened in 1838. Over the years it has gained quite a reputation as a luxurious place, many important personalities have stayed between the hotel’s burgundy walls.
The hotel also stands out on another front: it houses over 300 original artworks. Even though the changes of ownership and renovations lead to the first artworks in the hotel to be sold off in auctions, the amount of pieces has been restored with works of well-known artists.

Why?

Built near the Aarhus Cathredal and next to a farm, the hotel has officially opened its doors on 24th May 1883 as a place for “both travelers and the city's residents”.
Initially, Hotel Royal was more popular with travellers but after a while the higher citizens of Aarhus have gained a liking for it and it’s atmosphere.

Who?

Hotel Royal has been owned by Jens Richard Pedersen since 1983. Some of the artistic contributions to the hotel decor are being made by so-called “friends” of the hotel, for example Hans Krull.
There is also a continuous attempt to buy artworks and to hire more artists to work for the hotel.

When and where?

Opening hours: 24/7
Address: Store Torv 4, 8000 Aarhus
Transport access: Walking, autovehicle, micromobiles (eg: bike)
